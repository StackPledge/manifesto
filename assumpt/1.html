Assumption #1 (Demo Tool)

Facts relevant to this assumption

There are already some common denominators, because they are scientifically proven. So one does not have to research these basic facts again and again.

  • 1GB data transfer consumes 0,5kWh of energy.
  • 1kWh of energy produced by the current European electricity grid equals 0,295kg CO2

Assumption

Based on these facts the user now makes his own assumptions.

  • By being efficient with libraries and good code, you can reduce the size of a deployment on the web by 100kb.
  • If you have 2 million views per month, and 1 GB data transfer costs 0.5 kWh electricity, you save 0.5 kWh / 1,000,000 * 100 * 2,000,000 = 100kg CO2

Perspective

Is that a lot? Is it little? Since you can't do anything with the plain number, it makes sense to provide somecomparisons regarding the impact.

  • 100kg CO2 = Driving 692 km (with 158g CO2 per km of driving)
  • 100kg CO2 = 4 kg of beef. (at 25kg Co2)

Interpretation

Now sceptics could adapt the numbers to their situation, to what they assume, maybe in their particular setup, maybe based on their world view. This can help to understand where there is a lot of leverage (and where it is not worth discussing).
